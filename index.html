How will climate change affect the future generations?

Climate change will greatly affect future genrations by making the planet less safe and stable. As tempuratures continue to rise, extreme weather events like heatwaves, floods, wildfires, and hurricanes are expected to become more frequent and severe. This can damage homes, schools and infrustructure, putting lives at risk. Rising sea levels may flood costal areas, forcing people to leave their homes and leading to the loss of entire communities. Changes in climate can also make it harder to grow food and find clean water, leading to shortages and increase hunger. Health problems may increase due to pollution and the spread of diseases like malaria or heat related illnesses. In addition, many plants and animals may go extinct because they can't adapt fast enough, harming ecosystems and the natural balance of life. If action isn't taken soon, future generations could inherit a world that is much harder to live in.
